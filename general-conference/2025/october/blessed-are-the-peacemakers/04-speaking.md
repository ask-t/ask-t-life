# Step 4: Speaking Practice

> **学習目標**: 学んだ内容を自分の言葉で説明し、実生活に適用できるようになる

---

## Speaking Goal

このステップでは以下のことができるようになります：

1. **Explain（説明する）**: 話の内容を英語で説明する
2. **Share（分かち合う）**: 自分の経験と結びつけて話す
3. **Testify（証する）**: 学んだ真理について証する
4. **Apply（適用する）**: 実生活での行動計画を立てる

---

## Part 3: Bearing Testimony

### Exercise 4: Testimony Phrases

教会で証するときによく使うフレーズを練習しましょう：

#### Opening Phrases

<TextToSpeech text="I know that Jesus Christ is the Prince of Peace." />

- "I know that..."（〜であることを知っています）
- "I believe that..."（〜を信じています）
- "I have felt..."（〜を感じました）
- "I have learned that..."（〜を学びました）
- "I am grateful for..."（〜に感謝しています）

#### Testimony about Peace

<TextToSpeech text="I know that when we follow Christ's teachings, we can have peace in our hearts even during difficult times. I have felt His peace in my own life when I chose to forgive others." />

**Example:**
> "I know that when we follow Christ's teachings, we can have peace in our hearts even during difficult times. I have felt His peace in my own life when I chose to forgive others."

#### Testimony about the Talk

<TextToSpeech text="I am grateful for Elder Stevenson's message. I have learned that peacemaking is not weakness but strength. I want to be a peacemaker in my home and community." />

**Example:**
> "I am grateful for Elder Stevenson's message. I have learned that peacemaking is not weakness but strength. I want to be a peacemaker in my home and community."

#### Closing Phrases

- "I say these things in the name of Jesus Christ, amen."
- "In the name of Jesus Christ, amen."

## Part 4: Application & Action Plan

### The One-Week Peacemaker Plan

Elder Stevensonが提案した3つのステップを実践しましょう：

#### Step 1: Contention-Free Home Zone

<TextToSpeech text="This week, when I feel contention starting, I will pause and choose kind words instead. I will say things like 'I understand your point' or 'Let me think about that.'" />

**My commitment:**
> "This week, when ____________, I will ____________."

**Useful phrases:**
- "Let's take a break and talk about this later."
- "I hear what you're saying."
- "Help me understand your perspective."
- "I'm sorry. Can we start over?"

#### Step 2: Digital Bridge Building

<TextToSpeech text="Before I post anything online, I will ask myself: Will this build a bridge or create a wall? If it might cause contention, I won't send it." />

**My commitment:**
> "Before I ____________, I will ask myself ____________."

**Questions to ask:**
- "Will this build a bridge?"
- "Is this kind and true?"
- "Would I say this face to face?"
- "Does this invite the Spirit?"

#### Step 3: Repair and Reunite

<TextToSpeech text="There is someone I need to reach out to. I will contact them this week to apologize, listen, or simply reconnect. Relationships are more important than being right." />

**My commitment:**
> "I will reach out to ____________ to ____________."

**Phrases for reconciliation:**
- "I've been thinking about you."
- "I'm sorry for what happened."
- "I value our relationship."
- "Can we talk?"
- "I want to understand how you felt."

## Part 5: Discussion Questions

### For Personal Study

## Part 6: Final Practice

### Full Presentation (3-5 minutes)

以下の構成で、この話について**3-5分のプレゼンテーション**を練習しましょう：

1. **Introduction** (30 sec): Introduce the talk and speaker
2. **Main Points** (2 min): Explain the three key points
3. **Scripture** (1 min): Share one scripture that supports the message
4. **Personal Application** (1 min): Share how you will apply this
5. **Testimony** (30 sec): Bear your testimony
