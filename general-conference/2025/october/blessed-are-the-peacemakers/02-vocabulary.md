## Key Phrases（重要フレーズ）

### Phrase 1: Disagree without being disagreeable

<TextToSpeech text="Disagree without being disagreeable." />

| 要素 | 説明 |
|---|---|
| **disagree** | 意見が異なる |
| **without being** | 〜にならずに |
| **disagreeable** | 不快な、嫌な |
| **意味** | 意見が違っても、態度は穏やかに |

**練習**: 以下の文を声に出して読んでみましょう：
- "I disagree with your opinion, but I respect you."
- "We can disagree without being disagreeable."

### Phrase 2: Build bridges, not walls

<TextToSpeech text="Build bridges of cooperation and understanding, not walls of prejudice or segregation." />

| 要素 | 説明 |
|---|---|
| **bridges** | 橋（つながり） |
| **walls** | 壁（分断） |
| **cooperation** | 協力 |
| **prejudice** | 偏見 |
| **segregation** | 分離、差別 |

### Phrase 3: Children of God

<TextToSpeech text="In our congregations and our communities, may we choose to see one another as children of God." />

| 要素 | 説明 |
|---|---|
| **congregations** | 会衆、集会 |
| **communities** | 地域社会 |
| **see one another** | 互いを見る |
| **children of God** | 神の子 |
